Welcome to Holy Trinity Primary and Secondary School! A leading educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. With a rich history of success and a commitment to innovation, our school offers a diverse range of academic programs designed to meet the needs and interests of every student.Our dedicated faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom, ensuring a stimulating and supportive learning environment. Beyond academics, our school prides itself on providing a vibrant extracurricular experience, with a wide array of clubs, sports teams, and artistic opportunities for students to explore their passions. Our state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories, libraries, and technology resources. Join us at Holy Trinity Primary and Secondary School and embark on a transformative educational journey that will prepare you for success in University and beyond.
